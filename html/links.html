this is a list of different website i find cool or useful.

games

emulation

  • emulation general wiki - wiki for all information involving emulators and what emulators to use for different systems.
  • r/roms megathread - best place to look for roms.
  • myrient - website that focuses on archiving video game collections to keep them from becoming lost to time.
  • hshop - repository of 3ds content. only place to get 3ds content now that the eshop died.
  • cfw guide - list of guides to hack consoles and devices.
  • nopaystation - download ps3/psp/psv from sony's servers.
  • cemu cfw guide - guide to cemu and optimizing performance.
  • switch emulators guide - guide to set up yuzu/ryujinx optimally.

games (other)

  • khinsider - rips of video game music in mp3 and flac quality.
  • creaminstaller - creamapi frontend to crack dlc for legitimate games. great for paradox games.
  • howlongtobeat - how long does it take to beat a game? what about complete it?
  • before i play - before you play a game or when you're early in a game, you can check to see some stuff here. minor spoilers but useful if you dont like wandering aimlessly through a game and hating it.
  • pcgamingwiki - wiki that documents details about pc games and common errors to troubleshoot.

computers

linux

  • arch wiki - mainly documentation for arch linux, but is extremely useful for any distro and even windows, and is one of the best wikis out there.
  • pkgs.org - want to see if a piece of software is available in a distro's repos? use this tool.

computers

  • alternativeto - find alternatives to all the programs you use.
  • neocities - spiritual successor to geocities, people can make their own little personal websites there.
  • fmhy - guide to getting free things on the internet. very legal.

security

  • terms of service; didn't read - search a website and see the privacy and security grade of it.
  • privacyguides - sane guide to privacy (and security) on the internet.
  • have i been pwned? - has your email address been in a data breach? check here.
  • virustotal - are you unsure of a file you downloaded? this tool lets you upload it and checks like 50 different antiviruses on it.
  • canyoublockit? - test your adblocker to see if it works properly.
  • amiunique - test your browser to see if your browser is unique or not.
  • 2fa directory - does a certain site support two factor authentication? what type of 2fa does it support? is it secure? find out here.

other

  • no hello - don't say hello, just ask the question.
  • tineye - good reverse image search to check if images might've been altered.
  • thenounproject - free icons for projects.
  • supercook - allows you to select ingredients and then it'll spit out recipes based on the ingredients you have.
  • doesthedogdie - website to check if there's things you won't like in a movie or show like abuse, if spiders/snakes/bugs are in it, triggers like drugs/alchohol, gore, jumpscares and more.
  • library genesis - do you want free knowledge?
  • ptable - interactive periodic table.
  • zoro - free anime streaming. use an adblocker.
  • the useless web - if you want to websurf really weird and funny websites, here you go.
