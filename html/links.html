Links

This is a list of different website I find cool or useful.

Games

Emulation

  • Emulation General Wiki - Wiki for all information involving emulators.
  • r/Roms Megathread - a catalog for rom dumps.
  • Myrient - Erista's repository of roms that focuses on archiving video game collections to keep them from becoming lost to time.
  • hShop - Erista's repository of 3ds content. only place to get 3ds content now that the eshop died.
  • Custom Firmware Guides - A list of guides to hack different devices.
  • NoPayStation - A program that acquires PS3/PSP/PSV files from Sony's own servers.
  • Switch emulator guides - Guides to set up yuzu and ryujinx optimally. Also has links to other stuff.

Games (other)

  • KhInsider - Rips and releases of game soundtracks in lossy and lossless quality.
  • CreamInstaller - CreamAPI frontend to crack dlc for legitimately owned games.
  • HowLongToBeat - This website allows you to see how long it'll take to beat a game.
  • PCGamingWiki - A Wiki that documents details about various PC games and common errors to troubleshoot.

Computers

Computers

  • ArchWiki - Mainly documentation for Arch, but is extremely useful for any Linux distro and even windows, and is one of the best repositories of information out there.
  • AlternativeTo - Find alternatives to all the programs you use.
  • Neocities - spiritual successor to geocities, people can make their own little personal websites there.
  • FreeMediaHeckYeah - Page of "free" things on the internet.

Security

  • Terms of Service; DR - Search a website and see the privacy and security grade of the Terms of Service.
  • PrivacyGuides - Useful advice to privacy (and security) on the internet.
  • HaveIBeenPwned? - has your email address been in a data breach? check here.
  • VirusTotal - This tool lets you upload files to check for anything suspicious.
  • 2FA Directory - Does a certain site support two factor authentication? What type of 2fa does it support? Is it secure?

Other

  • no hello - don't say hello, just ask the question.
  • thenounproject - free icons for projects.
  • supercook - allows you to select ingredients and then it'll spit out recipes based on the ingredients you have.
  • doesthedogdie - website to check if there's things you won't like in a movie or show like abuse, if spiders/snakes/bugs are in it, triggers like drugs/alchohol, gore, jumpscares and more.
  • ptable - interactive periodic table.
  • AniWave - used to be 9anime.
  • the useless web - if you want to websurf really weird and funny websites, here you go.
